About Us

We strive for a more connected and versatile society.

Languages connect us. They allow our societies to share knowledge, emotions, and opinions. With the advent of technology, connecting with those across the globe (those with difference cultures) becomes increasingly easier every day. Unfortunately, despite our ability to reach those across the ocean, we do so with one caveat: the language barrier.

The language barrier prevents us from fully understanding and appreciating other cultures. At ________, we believe that technology can be used to break down this barrier. We believe that the most effective way to learn languages is not through exercises or translation apps but rather through interacting with fluent speakers.

In light of Canada's 150th anniversary, we were inspired to create a web application to celebrate Canada's diversity. We envision that _______ will help Canadians connect with eachother and learn that our cultural diversity is what makes us strong.
